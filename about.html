ABOUT

Synterlude was created by Lina Krasniqi as part of the Machine Unlearning seminar by Nick Schmidt. It explores the playful and experimental creation of sounds using AI tools. A key part of the project involves a process where generated sounds are listened to by different people, and their descriptions are then used as prompts for the next round of sound generation. This iterative approach produces sounds with a variety of interpretations and often surprising results. The website invites visitors to take part in this process themselves. On the first page, you can listen to sounds at random. On the next level, you interact by clicking on specific areas, which then navigates you to the final page: you listen to a sound, describe it, and then see the original prompt. In this way, you experience the same participatory and iterative process that shaped the creation of the sounds.
